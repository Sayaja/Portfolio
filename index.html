The project aims to improve an already existing music service focusing on the users’ needs and the experience when using it. The result is an addition to Spotify’s mobile app with simple, intuitive interactions and a clean look.

InnoEnergy

In co-operation with the company InnoEnergy, a group project created a visualization of the energy consumption and fuse capacities for real estate owners. The visualization helps the real estate owners to make insightful decision for the environment and lower costs. My role in the group was front-end programming, visual design and user experience.
